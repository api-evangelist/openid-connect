The OpenID Foundation has launched a new working group – the Digital Credentials Harmonized Presentation Working Group (DCHP WG) . The new DCHP WG supports a joint initiative between experts of ISO/IEC JTC1/SC 17 (ISO) WG10 and WG4 and the OpenID Foundation’s Digital Credentials Protocols Working Group (DCP WG) to harmonize their credential presentation protocols.
